Jimmy "The Rev" Sullivan

The man who change de drum world

Here's a time line of "The Rev" life:

  • 1981 - Born in Tustin, California
  • 1993 - His first drum set
  • 1999 - Avenged Sevenfold is born
  • 2001 - First album with Avenged Sevenfold (Sounding the Seventh Trumpet)
  • 2006 - The band won the MTV Music Award for Best New Artist, beating out Rihanna, Panic! at the Disco, Angels & Airwaves, James Blunt and Chris Brown
  • 2009 - Died at the age of 29

    • Jimmy “The Rev” Sullivan won various posthumous awards in 2010. He won Modern Drummer Reader’s Poll Award for “Best Metal Drummer”, a Revolver Golden God Award for “Best Drummer”, and was runner-up for Modern Drummer Reader’s Poll categories “Best Progressive Drummer” and “Best Recorded Performance” for Live In The LBC.
